LUNCHBOX STEPS

  • Lunchbox<<
  • Steps

STEP 1


Draw the schematic on your lunchbox exactly as shown in the picture above.

STEP 2


1. Start sewing the components.
2. Start with the microcontroller.
3. Start sewing from the negative pin of the microcontroller.
4. Sew through the negative lead three times to makes sure the stitches are tight..

STEP 3


1. Start from the negative lead of the microproceesor to the negative lead of the button.
2. Sew through three times.
3. Tie off the string and cut

STEP 4


1. Rethread the needle.
2. Tie a knot at the end.
3. Sew from the negative lead of the button to negatives of each of the lights in order from green yellow and red.

STEP 5


1. Sew into place the temperature sensor.
2. Temperature sensor is sewn onto the inside of the lunchbox as it has to sense the temperature from inside.
3. For sewing, follow the below steps:
  A. Sew negative pin of red LED to negative pin of the temperature sensor.
  B. Sew positive pin of the temperature sensor to A4 pin of microprocessor.
  C. Sew S pin of the temperature sensor to A5 pin of the microprocessor.

STEP 6


1. Now only the posiitve pins of the components are left.
2. Follow the below steps:
  A. S pin of button to 2 pin of microprocessor.
  B. Positive of green LED to 9 of microprocessor.
  C. Positive of yellow LED to 10 of microprocessor.
  D. Positive of red LED to 11 of microprocessor.
