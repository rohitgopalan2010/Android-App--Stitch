BRACELET STEPS

  • Bracelet<<
  • Steps

STEP 1


Test your LED and battery. The long leg of the Led should touch the side of the writing. It should light up.

STEP 2


Twist Led with pilers so you can sew it with your cuff.

STEP 3

Cut felt for cuff to wrap around wrist wiht 1 inch overlap.

STEP 4

Lay out components as below:

STEP 5

a. Sew the components. Before sewing, check that your parts match the picture. See the circuit only along the lines.
b. Sew from the prong snap to the LED +. Cut the thread.
c. Sew from the LED - to the battery - . Cut the thread.
d. Sew from the battery + to the hole snap. Cut the thread.

STEP 6

Test your cuff. It should turn it on when you snap it together.

STEP 7

Decorate your cuff! Sew pr glue felt scraps, beads and buttons. Draw with markers.
